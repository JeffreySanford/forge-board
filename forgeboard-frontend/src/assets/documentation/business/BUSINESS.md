## About ForgeBoard

### Overview

ForgeBoard is a sovereign monitoring and management dashboard with comprehensive data provenance tracking, secure in-house data management, and blockchain persistence. Developed by True North Insights, ForgeBoard provides organizations with complete control over their data while maintaining the highest levels of security and compliance.

### Company Background

**True North** is an American company specializing in enterprise software solutions with a focus on data provenance and security. Their mission is to provide technology that upholds American values of independence, innovation, and integrity.

**True North Insights** is the research and development division of True North, focused on creating innovative solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible with secure, compliant software. The team consists of leading experts in federal compliance, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

## Business Plans

### FedRAMP Market Opportunity

The Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program (FedRAMP) provides a standardized approach to security assessment, authorization, and continuous monitoring for cloud products and services used by the U.S. government. ForgeBoard is designed to meet and exceed FedRAMP 20X requirements, positioning it as an ideal solution for federal agencies and their contractors.

#### Market Analysis

- The federal government spends over $92 billion annually on IT, with a significant portion allocated to cloud services
- FedRAMP-authorized solutions have a competitive advantage in the federal marketplace
- The demand for compliant solutions has increased due to recent cybersecurity executive orders
- Agencies increasingly require solutions that provide complete data provenance and tracking

#### Value Proposition

ForgeBoard offers federal agencies:

1. **Complete Data Provenance**: Immutable tracking of all data changes, ensuring compliance with federal record-keeping requirements
2. **In-House Data Management**: Secure management of sensitive data without reliance on third-party cloud providers
3. **Comprehensive Security Documentation**: Ready-made security artifacts to streamline the Authority to Operate (ATO) process
4. **Blockchain-Based Audit Trail**: Immutable record of all data changes for compliance with federal record-keeping requirements
5. **Exceeding-Standards Compliance**: Meeting not just the minimum but exceeding FedRAMP 20X requirements

### Small Business Strategy

While ForgeBoard is enterprise-grade software, it's specifically designed to be accessible to small businesses seeking to enter the federal marketplace.

#### Small Business Value Proposition

1. **Reduced Compliance Burden**: Pre-built security documentation reduces the cost of achieving compliance
2. **Scalable Licensing Model**: Pricing structured to grow with the business
3. **Small Business Set-Aside Ready**: Documentation and technical architecture designed to support small business federal contracting requirements
4. **Competitive Advantage**: Enterprise-grade security at small business-friendly pricing

#### Small Business Success Path

1. **Entry Point**: Small businesses can begin with the core ForgeBoard platform for internal secure operations
2. **Growth Phase**: As the business secures federal contracts, ForgeBoard scales to meet additional compliance requirements
3. **Full Enterprise**: When ready, small businesses can deploy the complete ForgeBoard suite with all FedRAMP capabilities
